# Community Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

Aziky contributors and maintainers are committed to a welcoming, respectful,
and harassment-free community for everyone, regardless of experience, identity,
background, appearance, disability, nationality, or personal beliefs.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, patient, and constructive.
- Critique ideas and code rather than people.
- Give and accept technical feedback in good faith.
- Respect privacy and avoid publishing another person’s information.
- Acknowledge mistakes, correct them, and help others learn.
- Keep discussions relevant to the project and its community.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized attention, personal attacks,
trolling, deliberate disruption, sustained bad-faith argument, and publication
of private information are not acceptable. The same standard applies in issues,
pull requests, discussions, code review, chat, events, and other project spaces.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to Yassine Azily at
`yassine0171550@gmail.com`. Do not open a public issue containing sensitive
personal information.

Maintainers will review reports as promptly and impartially as practical. They
may edit or remove content; issue warnings; temporarily restrict participation;
or permanently ban participation when necessary. Maintainers must recuse
themselves from reports in which they have a conflict of interest.

Retaliation against a person making a good-faith report is prohibited.
